TotalSportek prioritises links that utilise Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABS). This technology allows the video player to detect the user's internet speed and device processing power in real-time. The platform indexes streams that employ the H.265 (HEVC) Codec. This modern video compression standard is vital for mobile streaming. H.265 allows TotalSportek links to deliver high-definition video data at half the bitrate of the older H.264 standard. For fans watching on smartphones or tablets via 4G/5G networks, this means crisper visuals and less data consumption. The H.265 (HEVC) Codec ensures that fast-moving sports, such as Formula 1 or Ice Hockey, retain clarity without pixelation (macro-blocking) during high-motion sequences.

Low Latency via WebRTC Protocols

Live sports require real-time delivery. TotalSportek favours sources that implement WebRTC Protocols. Reliability is ensured through Content Delivery Network (CDN) Peering. The links indexed by TotalSportek are often distributed across global CDNs.
